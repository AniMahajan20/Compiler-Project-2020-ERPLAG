ATTRIBUTES FOR PARSE TREE NODES :
syn  : astNode*
inh  : astNode*

2 types of semantic actions?
    - During upward traversal (upward propagation of syn attr). Free right subtree nodes accordingly.
    - During right/downward traversal (sideways/downward propagation of inh attr). Here free the appropriate left subtree nodes(only in case of sideways flow of inh)
